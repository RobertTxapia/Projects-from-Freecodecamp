Miguel de Cervantes

Trust in time, which usually gives sweet solutions to many bitter difficulties.

Here's a time line of miguel de cervantes life:

  • 1547 - Miguel de Cervantes was born in Alcalá de Henares, the son of a prosperous family with possible convert ancestors.
  • 1552 - He moved with his family to Valladolid.
  • 1566 - He moves to Madrid.
  • 1568 - He attends the classes of the humanist J. López de Hoyos.
  • 1569 - He published his first poems in the True Relationship of López de Hoyos. He enters the third of don Miguel de Moncada.
  • 1570 - Participate in the Battle of Lepanto.
  • 1575 - He falls prisoner of the Turks and spends five years in Algiers.
  • 1580 - He is released and returns to Spain.
  • 1584 - Premiere in Madrid of The deals of Algiers and Numancia . He married Catalina de Salazar y Palacios.
  • 1585 - He publishes the pastoral work La Galatea . He writes the first two comedies The Comedy of Confusion and Treaty of Constantinople and Death of Selim (both no longer exist).
  • 1587 - He entered the Academia Imitatoria, the first literary circle in Madrid. Designated royal commissioner of supplies.
  • 1603 - He lives in Valladolid.
  • 1605 - The first part of El ingenioso hidalgo don Quixote de La Mancha appears in Madrid . The second will do so in 1615.
  • 1613 - Publication of the Exemplary Novels.
  • 1614 - Publication of Viaje al Parmaso , work in verse.
  • 1615 - Publication of Comedies and appetizers .
  • 1616 - He dies in Madrid and is buried in the convent of the Barefoot Trinitarians.
